About Us

The UITS is the main provider of Information and Communications Technology (ICT) services across the University. It is responsible for the development and acquisition of ICT Infrastructure and services to drive the University’s core business of teaching, learning, research, and its related administrative activities. UITS offers IT services that meet the requirements of people and processes in the most cost-effective way. It is governed through the University’s Information and Communication Technology Management Committee and Subcommittee. They are responsible for the formulation of policies and regulations to guide the exploitation and use of ICT in the University. It also plays an advisory role to management on ICT-related issues.

Core Objectives

Extend ICT Services

To extend ICT services to all Units of the University.

User-Level Communication Services

To ensure the availability of User-level Communication Services.

Financial Sustainability

To develop schemes for the growth and financial sustainability of ICT resources through appropriate funding and operational mechanisms.

Sustainable ICT Management

To ensure sustainable management of the university’s ICT resources through the creation of appropriate institutional frameworks.

Content Management Systems

To develop content management and information systems for the University.

Training and Development

To regularly train all students and staff to equip them with the requisite skills to fully exploit the ICT environment in their respective functions.

Our Departments

Our specialized departments ensure top-tier service delivery.

Software Development

The software Development Department is primarily responsible for developing software required by the University depending on the required skill, time, and availability of such skilled personnel. They are also responsible for all support services that require changes to the structure of the software in question.
